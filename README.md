# Chicago Bike Demand Analysis: Weather & Event Impact

## Overview
Analyzed how weather and city events impact bike demand using Python, SQL, and Power BI to generate actionable business insights.

## Key Insights
- Demand drops ~30% during rainy conditions  
- Warm weather drives nearly 4x higher ridership than cold conditions  
- Events offset demand declines during poor weather  
- Casual riders are significantly more weather-sensitive than members  

## Tools Used
- Python (Pandas)  
- PostgreSQL  
- Power BI

## Business Recommendations
- Increase bike availability during warm weather and event-driven demand spikes  
- Adjust staffing based on weather forecasts to reduce inefficiencies  
- Target casual riders with promotions during high-demand conditions  
- Incorporate event schedules into demand planning strategies
